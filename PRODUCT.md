# WanderMind — AI Travel Planner

## App Identity
- Name: WanderMind
- Type: AI-powered personal travel agent web app
- Tagline: "Deskripsikan perjalananmu, kami yang rencanakan segalanya"

## Target Users
Travelers of all types — solo travelers, couples, families, backpackers — who want a personalized itinerary fast without hours of manual research. Indonesian and global market.

## Core Problem
Planning a trip manually takes hours: searching destinations, comparing hotels, finding activities, then assembling a day-by-day schedule. Users need an AI agent that does all of this in one conversation.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **AI Trip Planning Chat**: User inputs destination, budget (low/mid/high), travel dates, and interests (culture, food, adventure, etc.). AI generates a complete itinerary.
2. **Daily Activity Timeline**: Visual day-by-day schedule showing morning/afternoon/evening activities with time slots.
3. **Interactive Map**: Pinned locations for all recommended spots, hotels, restaurants on a map.
4. **Hotel & Attraction Recommendations**: Cards with name, brief description, price range, and rating.
5. **Itinerary Adjustment**: User can ask AI to modify plans ("less museums, more food spots", "cheaper hotels") and get updated itinerary.
6. **Save & Export**: Users can save their itinerary and export/share it.

## Brand Tone
Adventurous, warm, helpful. Like a knowledgeable friend who's traveled everywhere.

## Anti-patterns
- No overly complex booking flows in MVP
- No real payment processing
- No generic chatbot feel — should feel like a travel expert
