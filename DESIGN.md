# WanderMind Design Guidelines

## Visual Identity
- Theme: Modern travel + AI fusion
- Feel: Clean, adventurous, trustworthy
- Primary palette: Deep teal (#0d9488) + Ocean blue (#0369a1) gradient accents
- Background: Clean white with subtle warm tones

## Typography
- Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans — modern, bold
- Body: Inter — clean and readable

## Component Style
- Cards with soft shadows for hotel/attraction recommendations
- Timeline component: vertical day-by-day schedule with time markers
- Map: Full-width interactive map panel with location pins
- Chat interface: Side panel or bottom drawer for AI conversation
- Pill/badge components for budget level, interest tags

## Layout
- Split view: Left panel (chat/input), Right panel (map + timeline)
- Mobile: Tabbed navigation between Chat, Timeline, Map views
- Dashboard showing saved trips as cards

## Elevation & Depth
- Subtle card shadows
- Teal accent borders on active states
- Clean whitespace — not cluttered
